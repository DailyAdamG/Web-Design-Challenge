Latitude vs. Wind Speed

There is not much correlation in this chart between latitude and wind speed. Northern cities in the dataset have a higher wind speed than their southern counterparts. This could be because there are several cities between 60 and 80 degrees latitude, but no cites between -60 and -80 degrees latitude. It's possible that both poles of the earth could have higher wind speeds than the rest of the planet.
